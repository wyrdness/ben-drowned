## Verification Notes
BEN Drowned is a documented fictional creation by Alexander D. Hall (jadusable) in 2010. The creator is known, the production process has been explained, and it was always presented as a creative project combining storytelling with ARG elements. While the story is fiction, the project represents an important evolution in internet horror storytelling and ARG design.

---

**Research Guidelines**: Approach BEN Drowned as a case study in multimedia storytelling and ARG design. The story is acknowledged fiction that demonstrates how documented creative works can spread like folklore and influence internet horror culture. Focus on narrative innovation, cultural impact, and the intersection of gaming and horror rather than treating as potentially real phenomenon.
